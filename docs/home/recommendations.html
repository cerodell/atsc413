Recommendations for Other Courses

If you enjoyed ATSC 313, then here are some suggestions for introductory courses offered by the Earth, Ocean & Atmospheric Science Dept.

  • Online Courses at 1st Year. No prerequisites. Open to all students.

  • Online Courses at 3rd Year, specifically for students NOT majoring in Science or Engineering.

  • ATSC 201 - Meteorology of Storms.  An in-class course offered in Fall terms for science, technology, engineering & math (STEM) students.  Pre-req: Completion of first-year science.

  • ATSC 303 - Methods in Atmospheric Science.  An in-class course on Weather Instruments, offered in Spring terms.  Pre-reqs: EOSC 211 (or equivalent) and one of ATSC 201, GEOB 200, GEOS 200.

  • ATSC 313 - Renewable Energy Meteorology .  A hybrid course (all content is online and asynchronous, but the final exam is in person) offered in Spring terms for science, technology, engineering & math (STEM) students.  Pre-reqs: Completion of one course in each of first year math, physics and computer programming.

  • EOSC 326 - Earth & Life Through Time.  A hybrid course (all content is online and asynchronous, but the final exam is in person) open to all Science students except those majoring in Geology or Geophysics.

  • ATSC 413 - Forest Fire Weather & Climate .  An in-class course offered in Spring terms.   Pre-reqs: One of: ATSC 201, GEOS 200.
