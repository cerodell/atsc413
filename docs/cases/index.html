Wildfire Cases: An Overview

Under construction.

High Level, AB.  12 May - 18 Aug 2019

  • Name: "Chuckegg Creek fire"
  • Total burned area: 350,135 ha
  • 98 days out of control
  • Displaced over 3,000 residents from their homes.
  • Weather: strong low-level wind jet

Sparks Lake, BC.  June - July 2021

  • 15 km North of Kamloops Lake, BC.
  • 89,627.0 hectares burned.
  • Preceded by historic heat dome.
  • Weather: Spawned multiple pyro-thunderstorms with lightning that ignited many more fires.

Marshall, CO.  30 Dec 2021 - 1 Jan 2022

  • Boulder, CO, USA.
  • 6,026 acres (≈ 2,440 hectares) burned.
  • 1,084 buildings destroyed.
  • Weather: Strong downslope mountain winds ahead of a winter cold front that dropped a foot of snow.

Fort McMurray, AB.  1 May - 2 Aug 2016

  • 589,552 ha burned.
  • 88,000 people evacuated.
  • Weather: El Niño, hot, dry, windy.
